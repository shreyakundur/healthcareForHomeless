American Red Cross

The American Red Cross prevents human suffering human suffering in the face of emergencies with the help of volunteers and the generosity of donors. Red Cross provides shelter, food, and health care services to help less fortunate communities get back on their feet. It is an organization that takes pride in helping others no matter where they are located, regardless of race or religion.
